## Platform Overview

Xiaohongshu (RED / 小红书) is a Chinese social commerce and lifestyle discovery platform. The dominant content style is **种草** (zhǒng cǎo — "planting grass"), meaning soft, peer-to-peer product or service recommendation that reads like genuine personal discovery, not advertising.

Core demographic: urban Chinese women aged 18–35, high purchase intent, trust peer endorsement over brand advertising.

## Core Patterns

**Voice & perspective**
- Always first-person experience: "我最近发现…", "亲测有效", "用了半年终于来分享了"
- Brand is secondary — lead with the experience, the result, the story
- Authentic imperfection is fine; over-polished copy reads as fake and gets lower engagement

**Format**
- Title formula: Hook + Benefit + Emoji (e.g., "在澳洲落地只用了3天！跨境品牌必看✨")
- Body: short paragraphs, 1–2 sentences each, generous line breaks for mobile readability
- Emoji used throughout — not decoration, they signal tone and break up text
- 5–10 topic tags (#话题标签) at the end, on separate lines

**Post length**
- Sweet spot: 200–400 Chinese characters for body copy
- Title: 20–30 characters

**Visual**
- Carousel of 6–9 images performs best (3:4 portrait ratio, 1242×1660 px)
- Cover image carries most of the clickthrough weight
- Text overlay on cover image is normal (brand name, key benefit)

**Best posting times (CST)**
- Weekdays: 7–9 pm
- Weekends: 10 am–12 pm, 7–9 pm

## What to Avoid

- Hard-sell language: "立即购买", "限时优惠", "最低价格"
- Formal/corporate tone — reads as advertising, users scroll past
- Direct translations of English marketing copy — always write natively in Chinese
- Overt brand-first framing — lead with the human experience
- External links in content (XHS algorithm penalises off-platform redirects)

## Compliance (违禁词)

Xiaohongshu's content moderation is strict. Avoid:
- Medical/health efficacy claims without official 资质 (qualification/licence)
- Absolute superlatives: 最 (most), 第一 (number one), 唯一 (only), 最佳 (best)
- False or unverifiable performance claims
- Content that implies government endorsement
- Cooperation posts must carry 合作 or 广告 marker per platform rules
