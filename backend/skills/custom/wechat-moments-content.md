# WeChat Moments (朋友圈) Content Skill

## Platform Overview

WeChat Moments (朋友圈) is the personal social feed within WeChat. Even brand posts must read like a message from someone in your contact list — not a broadcast. The platform rewards intimacy and penalises obvious advertising.

Key constraint: **copy is hard-capped at 150 characters** before a "全文" (read more) link appears. Engagement drops sharply if users must tap to expand. Target under 120 characters for maximum impact.

## Core Patterns

**Voice**
- Conversational observation, micro-story, or question — not a pitch
- First or second person: "今天…", "你有没有想过…", "刚从Huntingdale回来"
- Occasional mild emoji (1–2 max) — unlike XHS, heavy emoji use on Moments feels out of place

**Length**
- Hard limit: 150 characters (including punctuation)
- Ideal: ≤100 characters
- Every character counts — cut ruthlessly

**Format**
- 1–9 images (the platform shows a grid; 9 images is common)
- Vertical portrait orientation preferred
- No hashtags — they do not function in Moments
- No external links in the post body (heavily downranked)

**Best posting times (CST)**
- 8–10 am (morning commute)
- 12–1 pm (lunch break)
- 9–11 pm (evening browsing)
- Frequency: max 1–2 posts per day from brand accounts

## What to Avoid

- Hard CTAs ("点击购买", "立即注册", "扫码报名") — off-platform conversion is penalised
- Long copy that requires "全文" — users rarely tap it
- Hashtags — they don't work on Moments
- External links visible in post body
- Overly promotional tone — users will mute the account

## Compliance

- 违禁词 rules apply identically to other Chinese platforms
- No political or religious sensitive content (auto-flagged)
- No financial solicitation or investment-related content
- WeChat Moments 广告 (paid Moments ads) follow separate WeChat Business rules — different content type from organic posts
